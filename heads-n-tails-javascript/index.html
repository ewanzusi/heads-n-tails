Heads N' Tails

Welcome to Heads N' Tails!

How to Play
1. Enter a Username
- Has to start with a capital letter.
- Must be at least 4 letters, maximum 8 letters.
- Cannot have numbers, spaces, or special characters.
- Each letter in the username must be alphabetically bigger
than the previous letter.

2. Guess and select which side you think shall be shown.
Either Heads or Tails.

3. Select on 'Start Game!' to start coin flip.
- Coin shall flip a random number of times between a minimum of 10 OR a maximum of 50 times!
- Number of flips will be shown and the current flip.

4. Once the Heads N' Tails game has completed, depending on your guess (Heads or Tails),
it will either display at least 1 Win or 1 Loss, if playing for the first time.
And if your guess is correct, of which side is shown by the coin after the flip
you will get a win. And if the side shown on the coin after the coin flip is different
from your guess you will get a loss.

5. To reset the game select on 'Reset Game' to play again.
You can optionally change your guess from Tails to Heads
OR from Heads to Tails before pressing 'Reset Game'.

6. Have fun! This game can be very addicting!
